Pittsburgh

Location

Pittsburgh is location near the Ohio-Pennsylvania border, south of Erie. It's surrounded by Appalachian foothills, giving a very winding and hilly topography.

About

Primanti Brothers

Primanti Brothers is a sandwhich shop and bar that originated in Pittsburgh. Their claim to fame is their sandwiches that have coleslaw on them, which in my opinion aren't that great.

The Rivers

Pittsburgh itself used to be a fortress, which was situated on the convergence point of 2 rivers which form a third river. The Allegheny and Monongahela combine to form the Ohio river.

The Pirates

The Pittsburgh Pirates aren't the greatest of the baseball teams in the MLB, but they used to be good. I think it was around 2012 that they upset and won a championship, but my memory of the time is fuzzy.
